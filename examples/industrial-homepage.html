Why JINHONG

Built to outlast the machine it controls.

Every JINHONG component is designed with a single intent: to be the last component you replace. Verified performance figures — not marketing claims.

IK10

Vandal-resistant

Stainless steel and hardened plastic housings rated to IK10 (20 J impact energy). Specified for public access panels, transport terminals, and high-abuse industrial environments.

1,000,000

Rated mechanical life

Each pushbutton and cam switch is rated for a minimum 1,000,000 mechanical operations under nominal load — factory-verified before shipment, per standard test protocol.

IP67

Dust-tight and jet-water resistant

IP67 sealing across the metal pushbutton and wireless switch product lines. Certified for washdown environments, outdoor-adjacent panels, and fine-dust industrial enclosures.

RF

Battery-free wireless

The WS-E generates its own transmit power from the kinetic energy of each press — no battery, no wiring, no scheduled maintenance. CE and RoHS qualified at launch.

Markets served

From elevator shafts to packaging lines.

JINHONG components are specified by machine builders and panel integrators across six demanding industries worldwide.

Elevators & Lifts

Floor selector panels, cabin emergency stop, shaft signal lights, and door-interlock pushbuttons. Suitable for EN 81-compliant cabin equipment.

Industrial Robotics

Machine guard interlock, palm switches, collaborative-robot E-stop, and status tower lights for robotic work cells and AGV charging stations.

Petrochemical

IP67 sealed and stainless-bodied pushbuttons for process areas where chemical resistance and reliable sealing cannot be compromised.

CNC Machining

Spindle start/stop, coolant control, door interlock, and five-tier alarm tower lights for machining centres, grinders, and EDM equipment.

Automotive Assembly

Assembly-line control panels, two-hand palm switches, wireless trigger stations, and line-status tower lights for body shop and trim lines.

Food & Packaging

Hygienic-grade stainless pushbuttons, IP67 indicator lights, and washdown-rated tower lights for conveyors, fillers, and cartoning machines.
