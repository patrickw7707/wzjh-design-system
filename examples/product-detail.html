LA22 Series Ø22mm Metal Pushbutton Switch

LA22系列 Ø22mm 金属按钮开关

Vandal-resistant Ø22mm pushbutton with stainless steel or zinc alloy body. Rated to 1,000,000 mechanical operations and sealed to IP67. Impact-rated IK10 for demanding environments including elevators, industrial robotics, petrochemical, and CNC machining.

Ordering guide

Head Type Variants

All variants share the same Ø22mm panel cutout, IP67 body, and modular contact block system. Specify head type, contact configuration, and body material when ordering.

LA22-10 / LA22-01 / LA22-11
Flush Head
  • Profile: flush with guard ring
  • Force: 2.5N–3.5N
  • Contacts: 1NO · 1NC · 1NO+1NC
  • Use: general control panels, accidental-actuation protection
LA22-20 / LA22-02 / LA22-21
Extended Head
  • Profile: raised 8mm above panel
  • Force: 2.5N–3.5N
  • Contacts: 1NO · 1NC · 1NO+1NC
  • Use: high-frequency stations, gloved operation
LA22-31 / LA22-32
Mushroom Head Ø40mm
  • Profile: Ø40mm cap, palm-operated
  • Force: 4.0N–5.5N
  • Contacts: 1NO+1NC · 2NO+2NC
  • Use: cycle-start, emergency-adjacent controls
LA22-41 / LA22-42
Illuminated (LED)
  • Profile: flush with integrated LED lens
  • Indicator: 24V · 110V · 220V AC/DC
  • Colors: Red · Green · Yellow · Blue · White
  • Use: status-feedback, selector indication
Custom configurations available. Body color, legend engraving, cable-entry termination, and explosion-proof (Ex) variants are available on request. Contact the JINHONG engineering team with your application parameters.
